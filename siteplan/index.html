Foodie Finds - Individual Project Site Plan

Site Name

Foodie Finds

Site Purpose

The site serves as a personal food blog where users can explore recipe ideas (from the Spoonacular API), view cooking instructions, learn what utensils and ingredients are needed, and save their favorite recipes to local storage. Visitors can also read about fictional restaurants and apply to join the food blogging team via a submission form.

Scenarios

  • What are some easy recipes I can try using ingredients I already have?
  • How do I prepare a dish from my favorite restaurant?
  • Can I save recipes to review them later?
  • How do I apply to become a contributor to the blog?

Color Schema

  • #FCD581 Primary color
  • #FFF8E8 Secondary color
  • #D52941 Accent color 1
  • #96062f Accent color 2

Typography

  • Lato Used for headings to give a stylized, gourmet feel
  • Open Sans Used for body text for readability and modern appearance
